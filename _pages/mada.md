[Mada Advances](https://carlottajarach.github.io/mada/) is a science-communication project founded in 2020 by a group of young female researchers who share a common goal: to explain the advances of science in language that is simple but never simplistic, always keeping an eye on how science relates to society.

Through in-depth articles on biology, medicine, pharmacology, the environment, technology and society, outreach on social media, interviews with researchers, science activities in schools, and a strong focus on women in STEM, with Mada we bring rigorous yet accessible science to a broad audience. I am part of the team as **Co-Founder and Editor in Chief**.
